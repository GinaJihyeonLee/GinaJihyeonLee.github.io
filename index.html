About Me

I am a MS student in DAVIAN Lab at KAIST Graduate School of Artificial Intelligence advised by Jaegul Choo. I received the Bachelor of Science in Biz&Tech Management and Industrial Engineering in 2020, at KAIST.

My current research interest is in improvement of large-scale language models. Formerly, I worked on projects that aim to learn de-biased representation, consider the personal style on machine translation, and solve efficient multimodal QA. More broadly, my research interests lies in the areas of robustness, fairness, multimodal learning, memory-augmented LM, and various natural language processing topics.
