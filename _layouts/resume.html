About Me

I am an AI Researcher of Kakaobrain, working in large-scale language model team. I received MS from DAVIAN Lab at KAIST Graduate School of Artificial Intelligence advised by Jaegul Choo. Before that, I obtained the Bachelor of Science in Biz&Tech Management and Industrial Engineering in 2020, at KAIST.

My research aims to improve the large-scale language models. I focus on two main topics; controllability of the generation to solve the toxicity problem and curation of large-scale dataset to effectively pre-train the language models. Formerly, I worked on projects that aim to learn de-biased representation, consider the personal style on machine translation, and solve efficient multimodal QA. More broadly, my research interests lies in the areas of controllable language generation, robustness and fairness of LMs, multi-modal learning, and various natural language processing topics.
